    Fee Circular 1 (2020-21)

    Dear Parents, 

    We sincerely hope that you and your family members are safe and healthy amidst this pandemic. Through the online teaching modules, our teachers are constantly giving their best efforts to help your child productively utilize the lockdown period. This is to ensure that the continuity in learning is not lost. 

    We extend our sincere thanks to all those parents who have paid the fee for the month of April during these difficult times, as your contributions has provided support to the school to function. We request other parents too, to pay the fee of April, as it will help us to maintain quality and also ensure that the school can function without any hindrance.

    We require the fee to meet the school's necessary expenses like salaries for the teaching and non-teaching staff, support and transport staff, housekeeping and security and other expenses like, government dues, electricity, telephones, insurance, EMIs and other obligatory disbursement. Hence, we request all our parents to deposit at the fee for the month of April at the earliest.

    We wish to make you aware that your school has always been taking fee in monthly instalments, which is in compliance with the state governmentâ€™s order of 7th April, 2020.

    Please note that due to the extended lockdown, transport fee has been temporarily removed from the fee payment schedule. Once the school reopens, we will re-access the transport fees applicable for session 2020-21and shall display it on the school website. However, if any transport fee has been paid by you then the amount will be adjusted in the transport fee once it is restructured post the lockdown.

    In case, if you are still finding it difficult to deposit the monthly fees, please notify us the reason, by filling and sending the application (https://bit.ly/3d0okvv) and send it to feedeferment@sunbeammughalsarai.com.

    We request you pay the fee by logging in on the School Website or Sunbeam School Mughalsarai Edunext app.

    I hope you understand the situation and support the school in managing its commitments as well as the 250+ staff members and their families who are solely dependent on the school for their livelihood.

    In case of any difficulty, please feel free to contact Mr. Ashwani, Administrator on his mobile 9838075639.

    Stay healthy and safe! 

    Regards,

    Principal 
    Sunbeam School Mughalsarai
