Sunbeam School Mughalsarai

Sunbeam School Mughalsarai, a dream child of a visionary par excellence Late Shri G.P. Kanudia is situated in a picturesque environment amidst serene surroundings on the G.T. Road, between Varanasi and Mughalsarai. A sapling planted in the year 2005, it has grown to its present magnificent form in a span of eight academic sessions. A Senior Secondary School affiliated to CBSE, Sunbeam Mughalsarai offers a curriculum with a holistic approach extending beyond the classroom. At +2 level, students may opt for the Science, Commerce or Humanities stream based upon their aptitude and goals.

Management Speaks

"Where the mind is without fear and the head is held high..."
This is my dream for our children. Our vision is to impart quality education to enlighten young minds, for only ignited minds can make our country progress. Our educational delivery system is an excellent blend of our traditional values and modern teaching technologies inspiring the children to dream... DREAM BIG, PLAN IT AND ACHIEVE IT. Believe in the incredible power of the human mind and children, I'm sure you will make a difference to this world, make it a much more happier and brighter place to live in.

Sweta Kanudia
Director

"Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man."
At Sunbeam School, Mughalsarai we keep in mind this very definition of education given by Swami Vivekanand and persevere continually to achieve the same for our students.Over the years, the school has evolved a pattern of holistic education which aims at developing the intellect, the physique and social consciousness of the growing child. Education is not the filling up of a pail, but the lighting up of a fire. At Sunbeam, we aim at providing an educational environment where students not only learn, but witness their learning turn into meaningful actions...

C.K.Palit
Principal

Sunbeam Mughalsarai has become synonymous with quality education today. When we envisaged Sunbeam Mughalsarai this is exactly what we had thought of and we are very happy that today Sunbeam Mughalsarai has been able to meet the standards that we had set for it. Now that the foundation has been laid, we would like to see it set new standards and our students to become our ambassadors, not only in Mughalsarai but all over. To achieve this, we will have to work very hard and we as the management will leave no stone unturned to provide the school with the best available in terms of infrastructure, resources and teachers...

Yaduraj Kanudia
Secretary
